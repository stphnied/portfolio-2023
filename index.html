About Me.

Download CV


Stéphanie Dang is a graphic designer based in Montréal, Canada. She completed a BFA in Computation Arts at Concordia University and a DEC in Multimedia Integration at Collège de Maisonneuve.

She specializes in poster creation and motion design, which has earned her 2nd place at the Exposition d’Affiches et de Concept de Jeux Vidéos édition 2017, organized by Collège de Maisonneuve. These expertises allowed her to intern at several organizations such as Simbioz, GFN Productions, and BetterSleep where she created wireframes, printed and digital advertising creatives including posters, and videos.
